Our team

Adriana Brkic

Founder and CEO. Adriana is a Grade 11 Student at Collège Jeanne-Sauvé in the LRSD School Division. Her passions include astronomy, biology, and youth education. Through her experience with such programs as CareerTrek, SHAD, and Duke of Edinburgh, she has learned about the importance of human connections and education. She hopes to use SYMB to pass these values along to the next generation of Winnipeg Youth.

Elijah Dietrich

Elijah Dietrich is a 16 year old junior in the International Baccalaureate program at Kelvin High school in Winnipeg, Manitoba. He enjoys studying STEM subjects and economics, and has a passion for finance. Elijah developed his interest in finance at a young age and has managed an investment portfolio since he was 14. He just completed an internship with the Director of Finance of a multimillion dollar restaurant company and has served as treasurer of his school’s student council, as well as its Youth In Philanthropy Committee. He is also the founding president of his school’s Environmental Action Committee which has completed many successful sustainability campaigns within his school and through which he organized a public forum with the Minister of Natural Resources. Elijah is currently blending his passion for finance and the environment by creating a stock index for the clean energy industry with a group of his friends. He has also blended his passion for the environment and science through research he conducted at the University of Manitoba on the leading methods of chemical solvent extraction of lipids from algae for biofuel production, by travelling to the Canadian arctic to conduct climate change, sustainability, and polar bear field research for ongoing research at the University of Manitoba, and is currently working on a design for a portable clean energy system. For his environmental and humanitarian efforts, Elijah received the YMCA-YWCA Peace Medallion in 2015. This summer, Elijah will attend SHAD at the University of British Columbia and will intern with Dr. Blair Jamieson , Professor of Physics at the University of Winnipeg who was involved in the 2015 Nobel Prize for physics, to assist with his research which seeks to understand how electromagnetic fields affect the quantum spins of neutrons. In his spare time, Elijah rows crew with the Winnipeg Rowing Club, runs varsity cross country, writes for his blog which seeks to educate others on the financial system and how to invest, and can often be found in a local coffee shop. He aspires to one day become a venture capitalist, business magnate, and politician and is a proud supporter of Bernie Sanders. Elijah believes that entrepreneurship is the best route to innovation, which is so essential to human progress, now more than ever. He also believes that his generation will be the one to realize this progress and is thrilled to be the first step in this process through Startup Youth Manitoba.
